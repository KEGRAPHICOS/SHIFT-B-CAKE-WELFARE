RULES & REGULATIONS

CONTRIBUTION GUIDELINES & ELIGIBILITIES

  1. Members must be regular contributors to be eligible for any benefit.
  2. Advance payments are allowed.
  3. Members are entitled to a maximum of two benefits per year.
  4. New members must contribute for a minimum of four months before accessing any benefits (backpay is allowed).
  5. Any member who misses a month's contribution will forfeit all welfare benefits.
  6. All monthly contributions must be made promptly after salary payments.
  7. Contributions will be accurately recorded by the Welfare Treasurer.

CONDUCT & DISCIPLINE

  1. All members must uphold mutual respect and honesty.
  2. Any form of misconduct, false reporting, or internal conflict may result in sanctions.
  3. Grievances should be addressed through the Welfare Committee for resolution.
