POLICIES

BENEFIT CLAIM PROCEDURE

  1. A formal request with relevant documentation (e.g., invitation, medical report, death certificate) must be submitted.
  2. The Welfare Committee will review and process claims within 5 working days.
  3. False claims will result in automatic expulsion from the group.

WITHDRAWAL/EXIT POLICY

  1. Members may officially resign by notifying the Welfare Committee.
  2. Members who have contributed for more than a year will receive 70% of their total contributions.
  3. No post-resignation benefits apply to members with less than a year of contribution.
